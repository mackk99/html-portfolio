Cameron MacKinnon's Favourite Shows

My top 3 favourite shows of all-time

Lost

My favourite show of all time is lost, it has mystery, interesting plot twists and things you would never expect.

Prison break

Prison break has a lot of unique characters some you will hate, some you will love, but the show is very unique and something you wont find elsewhere.

Daredevil

Daredevil is a Marvel show with a superhero with unique abilities he is a regular human who was blind as a child due to an accident however this made his other senses heightened, he's able to hear people's heartbeats to know if they're lying, hear people through walls or down the street, it's a good watch.
